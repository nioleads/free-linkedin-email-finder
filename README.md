## Features

By identifying potential prospects in relevant groups and industry affiliations, you can efficiently connect with interested individuals, improving your lead generation efforts.

Networking with attendees at local events can help you connect with a targeted audience in the relevant industry, expanding your potential lead sources and facilitating business growth.

The tool also allows you to extract commenters and likers from posts, enabling you to retarget users based on feed engagement to further expand your lead generation efforts.
